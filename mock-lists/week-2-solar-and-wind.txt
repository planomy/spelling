# Week 2 — Solar & Wind Power (Year 5 Renewable Energy Unit)
# Paste everything below the line into SpellQuest

1. solar - relating to the sun and its energy
2. photovoltaic - describing panels that turn sunlight directly into electricity
3. panel - a flat device, often on a roof, that collects sunlight or converts it to power
4. turbine - a machine with spinning blades that uses moving air or water to make electricity
5. windmill - an old-fashioned structure that uses wind power, often to grind grain
6. blade - the long flat part of a turbine that catches the wind and spins
7. rotate - to turn around in a circle
8. convert - to change something from one form into another, such as sunlight into electricity
9. efficient - working well without wasting much energy
10. installation - the process of setting up equipment such as solar panels or wind turbines
