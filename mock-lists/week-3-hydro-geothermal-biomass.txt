# Week 3 — Hydro, Geothermal & Biomass (Year 5 Renewable Energy Unit)
# Paste everything below the line into SpellQuest

1. hydroelectric - electricity made by using the force of moving water
2. dam - a wall built across a river to hold back water and create power
3. geothermal - heat energy that comes from deep inside the Earth
4. biomass - organic matter such as plants and waste used as a fuel source
5. combustion - the process of burning something to release heat energy
6. emission - gas or pollution released into the air, often from burning fuels
7. reservoir - a large stored supply of water held behind a dam
8. tidal - relating to the rise and fall of ocean tides, which can be used to generate power
9. decompose - to break down naturally over time, as plants and food waste do
10. methane - a gas produced when organic waste breaks down; it can be used as fuel
