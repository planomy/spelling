# Week 4 — Environment & Report Writing (Year 5 Renewable Energy Unit)
# Paste everything below the line into SpellQuest

1. environment - the natural world around us, including air, water, land, and living things
2. carbon-dioxide - a gas released when fossil fuels are burned; it contributes to climate change
3. greenhouse - describing gases that trap heat in the atmosphere and warm the planet
4. climate - the usual weather patterns of a place over a long period of time
5. pollution - harmful substances that damage the air, water, or land
6. alternative - another choice or option, such as using renewable energy instead of coal
7. investigate - to find out facts by researching and asking questions
8. conclusion - the final part of a report where you sum up what you have found
9. paragraph - a group of sentences about one main idea in a report
10. subheading - a smaller title that organises information in a report
